The approach

Reputation is
everything.

Great PR isn't about spin - it's about telling your real story in the most compelling way possible. Whether you're launching a new product, managing a crisis, or building long-term brand authority, I help you take control of the narrative.

With experience across the UK and Canadian media landscapes - including broadcast partnerships with Bell Media affiliates - I know how to get your story in front of the journalists, producers, and audiences that matter most to your business.

What I offer

PR that moves

Press Releases

Media Relations
Building and leveraging genuine relationships with journalists, editors, and producers across print, digital, and broadcast.
Broadcast PR
Securing TV and radio coverage for your brand - from interview bookings to co-ordinating broadcast segments and features.
