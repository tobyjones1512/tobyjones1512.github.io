The approach

Editing that serves
the story.

Great editing is invisible. Whether I'm cutting a narrative feature, shaping a 30-second commercial, or assembling a week's worth of social content, the job is always the same - find the story in the footage and tell it as cleanly and compellingly as possible.

I work in Final Cut Pro for narrative and commercial projects, and OBS for live YouTube broadcasts. From the offline assembly to the final colour-corrected export, every cut is intentional.

How it works

From raw footage
to final cut.

01
Brief
We discuss the project - format, tone, intended platform, and deadline. I'll flag anything I need from you before the footage arrives.
02
Offline Edit
I cut the assembly in Final Cut Pro - building structure, pacing, and narrative from your raw material. You review and give notes.
03
Refinement
I incorporate your feedback and lock the picture - adding graphics and colour correction to the agreed standard.
04
Delivery
You receive the finished file in your required format and specs - broadcast-ready or platform-optimised, exactly as agreed.
