Original Screenplay

Vengeance

After witnessing a gruesome crime, Cait plots to get vengeance on the criminals involved.

Six months before the opening frames of their student slasher film roll, Mike and his crew commit a violent crime to fund their passion project - and Cait sees everything. A quiet, overlooked member of the group, she bides her time. When production begins, she is right there on set - boom mic in hand, mask waiting. One by one, the people who were there that night will answer for what they did. Blending the self-aware language of meta-horror with a slow-burn revenge thriller, Vengeance asks who the real monsters are when the camera stops rolling.

Dangerous Woman

One click. One mistake. Everything to lose.

Based on true events from 2018, Peter is a young man newly dumped and looking for connection - who stumbles into a late-night catfishing trap on a video chat site. By morning, explicit images have been sent to his friends and family and he faces a $2,000 extortion demand. As shame and desperation spiral out of control, Peter finds himself standing on a bridge with nowhere left to turn. A raw, unflinching short film about the very real psychological devastation of online sextortion.
