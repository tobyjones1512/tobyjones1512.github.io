The Process

Models

Please download the model casting sign-up form here and complete it digitally. Once complete, send it as a PDF attachment (not an online link) to model.audition@tobyjones.info. Should you have a portfolio, please attach it as a PDF (though a link will be accepted if it is online). Use the following format for the subject line:

MODEL AUDITION - [NAME HERE]

Use the body of the email to introduce yourself. Unsolicited model auditions are accepted. Good luck!

Actors

Casting calls for actors are held on Backstage. No unsolicited actor auditions will be accepted.

Voice Cast

Casting calls for voice roles are held on Backstage. No unsolicited voice actor auditions will be accepted.
