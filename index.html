Toby R. Jones

Screenwriter, Director, Video Editor & Web Designer

I am a Whitby (Toronto) based filmmaker and web designer driven by the belief that every story deserves to be told with precision and impact. My journey in media began at The Blackpool Sixth Form, where I developed a foundation in Film & TV Production, eventually leading me to the renowned New York Film Academy (NYFA). At NYFA, I honed my craft in directing, editing and cinematography, collaborating with global crews and learning to translate complex ideas into cinematic reality.

With a background that spans social media management, web development and post-production, I specialize in creating content that doesn't just look good—it resonates. Whether I’m directing on-set or refining a narrative in the edit suite, my focus is on merging technical proficiency with creative intuition to help brands and creators find their unique voice.

Proficient using: Final Cut Pro, Premiere Pro, Photoshop, Final Draft and WordPress.

Camera Operation / 1st Assistant AC

Script Doctor / Supervisor

I bridge the gap between page and screen by refining dialogue for maximum impact and ensuring flawless continuity. I keep the details consistent so the audience stays fully immersed in your narrative.
